Grigory Bartosh

Ph.D. Student at the University of Amsterdam

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in the Amsterdam Machine Learning Lab (AMLab) at the University of Amsterdam, supervised by Christian A. Naesseth. Previously, I completed my Master’s in Data Science at the Higher School of Economics, under the supervision of Dmitry Vetrov.

I work in the field of Deep Learning, with research interests focused on Generative Models, particularly Diffusion Models.
